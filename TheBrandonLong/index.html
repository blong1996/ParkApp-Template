About

Originally from Stone Mountain, GA, Brandon is studying Computer Science at North Carolina A & T State University in Greensboro, NC. During the school year, he can be found in his dorm working on a random new project, in class learning more about programming, or on the practice field playing his French Horn as a member of the Blue & Gold Marching Machine. At the end of the Spring semester, though, he is packing his bags and headed to new places to work with top companies on projects meant to change the world. His ultimate goal in life is to leave behind something that will always be useful to users for generations to come.
